Exports

Afghanistan also has a few partners for exporting. These include India, Pakistan, and Tajikistan in order from greatest to least precentage of exports. Afghanistan's exports gives Afghanistan around $660 million. Afghanistan's main exporting products are dry fruits and fresh fruits, nuts, Afghan rugs, wool, cotton, hides and pelts, gemstones as well as saffron.

Imports

Afghanistan has many partners for importing as most of the country's necesseties are imported from countries around the world. These include Pakistan, India, United States, China, Kazakhstan, and Azerbaijan in order from greatest to least precentage of imports. Afghanistan's imports cost around $6.2 billion. Its main importing products are machinery and other capital goods, food, textiles, and petroleum products.
